Executive Summary

At 02:17:43 UTC on 15 May 2025, the GridWatch CSOC PBAD engine flagged an anomalous outbound connection from IoT-CAM-01 (IP Camera 01, 192.168.10.21) located at the perimeter entrance of the Al Noor Government Services Campus.

IoT-CAM-01 is configured to stream video exclusively to the internal SIEM server at 192.168.30.30. Instead, it initiated a high-volume outbound data transfer to an unrecognised external IP address during a period of zero scheduled activity. This behaviour directly violated the established PBAD baseline and triggered a CRITICAL alert.

The GridWatch CSOC network segmentation design contained the incident to Zone 1, preventing any access to the secure government network in Zone 3. PBAD detected the anomaly within two seconds. Full containment was achieved in four minutes.
